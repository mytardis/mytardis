About

Store.Monash assists researchers in storing, managing, sharing and disseminating data generated by scientific instruments.

For more information about MyTardis project in general, please visit mytardis.org

Please login to see your experiment data.

Statistics

Since deployment in 2001 Store.Monash have ingested:

  • 2.2PB of data
  • 33,487,075 files
  • 81,059 datasets
  • 8,693 experiments

This data has been uploaded from 136 instruments by 907 users.

Facilities

Major facilities at Monash university trust their data to Store.Monash including:

  • BioEM, 1.2PB
  • Monash Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences, 409.2TB
  • Monash Micro Imaging, 295.9TB
  • NY79, 136.8TB
  • Monash Biomedical Proteomics Facility, 75.1TB
  • SF32, 43.7TB
  • XMFIG Facility, 27.1TB
  • MBI, 20.5TB
  • Flow Core, 6.1TB
  • ARA Preclinical Imaging, 4.6TB

Instruments

We support any instrument as soon as it has Windows or Linux installed.
